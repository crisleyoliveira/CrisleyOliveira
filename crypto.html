Introduction

The realm of video games is a captivating universe that has enthralled millions of players worldwide. From epic adventures to immersive storytelling, video games offer a unique form of entertainment and escapism. Behind the scenes, however, lies a complex web of data that holds the keys to understanding the industry's dynamics. In this data analysis article, we embark on a journey to unlock the secrets hidden within the numbers, exploring the intriguing relationship between critical reception, user satisfaction, and commercial success.

As players, we often find ourselves debating the merits of a game based on its Metascore rating or engaging in lively discussions about our favorite titles. But have you ever wondered how these ratings truly correlate with the popularity and success of a game? Are highly-rated games always the best-selling ones? And do different gaming brands and platforms have a significant impact on these dynamics? Prepare to have your preconceptions challenged and your curiosity satiated as we unravel the mysteries that lie beneath the surface of the gaming industry. The journey awaits, and the answers lie within the numbers.

In this project, we will analyze the top 100 video games of all time based on their rating, popularity, and release year. The data set used for this project will be obtained from a reputable online video game database. The aim of this project is to identify patterns and trends among the top-rated video games and to uncover insights that can help inform game development and marketing decisions.
